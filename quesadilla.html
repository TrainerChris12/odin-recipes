Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish of a folded tortilla filled with cheese and other ingredients, then cooked until golden brown. The word "quesadilla" translates to "little cheese thing" in Spanish.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla, traditionally made of corn but can also be flour
  • Cheese, usually melted
  • Meat, such as chicken, beef, pork, veal, or turkey
  • Vegetables, such as onions, bell eppers, black beans, or corn
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat
  2. Place a tortilla in the skillet and top with cheese and your desired filling.
  3. Tops with another tortilla and cook for about 2 minutes per side, or until the tortillas are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  4. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling. Serve warm.
