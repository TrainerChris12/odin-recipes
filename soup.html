Soup

Description

Soup is a liquid-based dish made by simmering ingredients such as vegetables, meat, fish, or legumes in a flavorful broth or stock. It can be served hot or cold and is suitable for any season.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Beans
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Heat two tablespoons oil in a stock pot over medium heat.
  2. Saute the onion, carrots, celery and garlic until softened about five minutes.
  3. Add the herbs, bay leaf.
  4. Add the beans.
  5. Serve!
