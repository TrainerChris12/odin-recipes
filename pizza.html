Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients. It is typically baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the dough to your desired thickness and shape
  3. Spread the pizza sauce on the dough.
  4. Add your preferred toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions).
  5. Sprinkle with cheese and dried herbs.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven until crust is golden and toppings are cooked.
